    Home

    My name is Pablo, Computer Engineering student at the University of Deusto. I like working on low-level programming with C/C++, and also work extensively with Python, driven by a strong interest in mathematics and machine learning.

    This website documents the Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions in which I have participated as a member of 0xD3C0D3 (Deusto Electronic Club Of Developers and Engineers). Only some writeups are published, focusing on those challenges that present particularly interesting solutions or insights.
